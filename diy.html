Homemade Recipes for Your Pets

Show love to your furry friends with easy, healthy, and fun treats.

Why Make Homemade Treats?

Homemade treats are the best way to ensure your pets are eating wholesome and nutritious snacks. By making treats at home, you can choose healthy, pet-safe ingredients and tailor recipes to suit your pet’s taste and dietary needs.

  • Customizable: Adjust recipes for allergies or preferences.
  • Healthier Ingredients: Use only high-quality, pet-safe ingredients.
  • Bonding Time: Cooking for your pet is a wonderful way to show you care.

DIY Recipe: Peanut Butter and Banana Bites

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 tablespoons of Jay’s Pet Butter (Original or Peanut-Free)
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  1. Mash the banana in a bowl.
  2. Add Jay’s Pet Butter and rolled oats, mixing until combined.
  3. Roll into small bite-sized balls.
  4. Place on a tray and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.

Serving Suggestion: Serve as a snack or reward for good behavior.

Storage and Serving Suggestions

Homemade treats lack preservatives, so proper storage is essential:

  • Store baked treats in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 7 days.
  • Freeze larger batches for up to 3 months.
  • Always thaw frozen treats before serving.
